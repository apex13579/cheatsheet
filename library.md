# Full-Spectrum Proficiency: The Strategist's Reading List

This repository tracks the literature and study materials required to bridge the gap between technical execution (A.S./Certifications) and strategic leadership (MSIT/Career Maneuvering).

---

## 🎓 Phase 1: Academic & Certification Path
*Ordered by compounding priority for the TSC-to-FSU transition.*

### 🛠️ The Foundation (Semester 1-2)
*Focus: Clearing prerequisites and establishing logic.*
- **College Algebra Essentials** – Robert Blitzer 
- **Python Crash Course** – Eric Matthes
- **Python Institute Free Self-Paced Training** – Edube Interactive
- **Storytelling with Data** – Cole Nussbaumer Knaflic (Preparation for Tableau/Kaggle integration)

### 🛡️ The Technical Core (Semester 3-4)
*Focus: Linux, Networking, and the Ethical Hacking Core.*
- **The Linux Command Line** – William Shotts
- **CompTIA Linux+ Study Guide** – Christine Bresnahan
- **CCNA 200-301 Official Cert Guide Library** – Wendell Odom
- **31 Days Before Your CCNA Exam** – Allan Johnson
- **Computer Networking: A Top-Down Approach** – Kurose & Ross
- **CEH v12 Certified Ethical Hacker Study Guide** – Ric Messier
- **The Basics of Hacking and Penetration Testing** – Patrick Engebretson
- **RTFM: Red Team Field Manual** – Ben Clark

### 🏛️ The FSU BSIT/MSIT Bridge
*Focus: Systems design, Information Architecture, and graduate-level theory.*
- **Information Architecture for the Web and Beyond** – Louis Rosenfeld
- **Network Security Essentials** – William Stallings
- **Project Management Case Studies** – Harold Kerzner
- **Systems Analysis and Design** – Alan Dennis
- **CompTIA PenTest+ Study Guide** – Mike Chapple (Supplemental to CEH for broad coverage)

## ♟️ Phase 2: Career Maneuvering & Strategy
*Categorized by psychological and professional "Skill Trees."*

### ⚔️ The Strategist's Arsenal (Robert Greene)
*The rules of the board and the psychology of power.*
- **Mastery** – Robert Greene (The Blueprint for the Apprenticeship Phase)
- **The 48 Laws of Power** – Robert Greene (Defensive/Offensive awareness)
- **The 33 Strategies of War** – Robert Greene (Organizational and competitive strategy)
- **The Laws of Human Nature** – Robert Greene (Decoding colleague and manager motivations)
- **The 50th Law** – Robert Greene & 50 Cent (Developing professional audacity)
- **The Art of Seduction** – Robert Greene (Mastering professional allure and buy-in)
- **The Daily Laws** – Robert Greene (Maintaining strategic focus)
- **The Law of the Sublime** – Robert Greene (Expected 2026 - The aesthetics of mastery)

### 🗣️ Tactical Empathy & Negotiation (Chris Voss / Black Swan)
*High-stakes communication for salary, roles, and technical buy-in.*
- **Never Split the Difference** – Chris Voss
- **The Full Fee Agent** – Chris Voss & Steve Shull (Defining and defending your professional value)
- **Ego, Authority, Failure** – Derek Gaunt (Leadership for technical teams)
- **Beacons of Leadership** – Chris Voss (Scaling tactical empathy into authority)
